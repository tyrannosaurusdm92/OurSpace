What this final app includes

Socials Application is a clean open-source friend-network app built from the safe, useful parts of the merged Discord, Snapchat-style, and Zoom-style packages.

  • Friend feed with posts, media, comments, and emoji reactions.
  • Discord-like channels with JSON history import/export.
  • Snap-style camera, filters, overlays, local gallery, and 24-hour stories.
  • Zoom-style private rooms with WebRTC, screen share, chat, reactions, notes, transcript import, and exports.
  • Native retro footer messenger plug-in with MySpaceIM-style friends, Facebook-style bottom chat tabs, files, images, GIFs, stickers, camera snapshots, voice notes, and up-to-10-person call signaling.
  • Integrated offline mobile games with a messenger plug-in presence roster for multiplayer-capable games.
  • No token scraping, account automation, cracking/brute forcing, leaked-client code, or third-party service impersonation in the runnable app.

Full manifests, audits, and license notes are in the included docs folder.
