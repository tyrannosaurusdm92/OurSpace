Tiny version

Access notes
  • Use eyes-open grounding if closing eyes feels unsafe.
  • Use quiet, low-scent, low-touch options for MCAS, trauma, and sensory overload.
  • No posture requirement; lying down or supported sitting is valid.
Us notes
