Mindfulness of Current Thoughts Due Date: Name: Week Starting: Describe your efforts to observe your thoughts in the past week. Practice observing thoughts each day at least once. Don’t focus just on thoughts that are painful, anxiety-provoking, or full of anger; also observe and be mindful of pleasant or neutral thoughts. For each thought, first practice saying, “The thought [describe thought] went through my mind.” Then practice one or more strategies to observe and let go of thoughts.
